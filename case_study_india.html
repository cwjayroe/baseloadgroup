Market Entry Risk Assessment - India

The Challenge

A U.S. multi-national was considering opening a production facility in one of three potential locations in India. In particular, they needed to know the estimated costs to provide physical security and secure intellectual property.

Our Solution

“We conducted a comprehensive assessment of the risks associated with constructing and operating a production facility at one of several potential locations in India.”

Baseload Group began with a Risk Needs Assessment to quantify and articulate the client’s concerns and requirements. We then produced an All Perils Profile of the proposed locations to establish an accurate picture of the operating environment including security threats, natural hazards and other potential barriers such as cultural norms, government legislation or other actions that might impact operations. Finally, we made recommendations to mitigate their risks including physical security measures, crisis management, cyber security, emergency response, as well as business continuity plans, policies, and procedures.


Baseload Group is a full-service risk management company. We enable organizations and individuals to operate safely and effectively, both domestically and around the globe.
