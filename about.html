About Baseload

“Our purpose is to enable people and organizations to safely go further and do more.”

Baseload Group is a full-service risk management solutions company headquartered in Jacksonville, FL since 2014.

Our team provides situational awareness, assessments for strategic and operational decision-making, reach-back capability for assistance and rapid response, discreet security solutions, specialist training, products, and expeditionary logistics. Utilizing a risk-based approach, Baseload delivers tailored, personal consultation and reporting for each client’s unique needs.

Baseload Group’s management is comprised of a cohesive team of business and military leaders with decades of experience in U.S. and U.K. elite military organizations; the U.S. Secret Service; and organizations with logistics, security, risk management, medical assistance and crisis response experience.

The origins of Baseload Group date back to 1999 when co-founder Jack Stradley helped to develop a high-risk environment training and security operations company based in northern Virginia which was later acquired by Kroll, Inc. In 2014 he founded ExLog Global, LLC with an immediate focus on providing corporate clients with security and logistical support in challenging environments in the Middle East and North Africa.

In the fall of 2018, ExLog leadership realized that the exponential growth in the marketplace demanded a more robust offering. With that, ExLog was acquired by the Baseload Group to provide expanded services to clients worldwide.
