About Exlog

“Our purpose is to enable people and organizations to safely go further and do more.”

Exlog is a full-service risk management solutions company headquartered in Jacksonville.

Our team provides situational awareness, assessments for strategic and operational decision-making, reach-back capability for assistance and rapid response, discreet security solutions, specialist training, products, and expeditionary logistics. Utilizing a risk-based approach, Exlog delivers tailored, personal consultation and reporting for each client’s unique needs.

Exlog’s management is comprised of a cohesive team of business and military leaders with decades of experience in U.S. and U.K. elite military organizations; the U.S. Secret Service; and organizations with logistics, security, risk management, medical assistance and crisis response experience.

The Exlog Difference

Exlog employs a risk-based approach to crafting adaptable action plans that identify and mitigate risk to our client’s global business operations. Our people have the experience and courage needed to make the tough decisions that keeps your organization and your people safe during a crisis. From the top down, we will hold ourselves to nothing but the highest ethical and professional standards; remaining accountable for delivering on our promises.
