Active Shooter Security - Tactical Response Team

The Challenge

A large, industry-leading company grew concerned about the escalating risk environment potentially impacting their corporate HQ and employees. Lack of an integrated security team and concerns with official response times led them to seek our assistance.

Our Solution

“This scalable program enabled our client to harden access to their HQ, maintain readiness to respond and protect people. ”

Exlog conducted an assessment to identify the organizational risk profile and security vulnerabilities. We paid particular attention to active shooter and other violent threats. Once complete and reviewed with the client, we developed a project plan to build a security program which included resourcing and equipping a tactical response team certified to meet state licensing standards, procedures, ongoing capability reviews and in-service training.


Exlog is a full-service risk management company. We enable organizations and individuals to operate safely and effectively around the world.
