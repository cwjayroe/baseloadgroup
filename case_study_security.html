Security Operations Center and Protective Intelligence

The Challenge

In 2017, our multibillion-dollar Silicon Valley-based client identified the need for a security operations center to support threat, vulnerability, and risk assessments, protective intelligence, and travel security.

Our Solution

We assisted the client with the design and establishment of a security operations center and protective security program. We recruited, trained and established a 15-person team composed of highly experienced intelligence analysts and investigators that provide around-the-clock protective intelligence and investigations, consulting, and security monitoring and response services.


Exlog is a full-service risk management company. We enable organizations and individuals to operate safely and effectively, both domestically and around the globe.
